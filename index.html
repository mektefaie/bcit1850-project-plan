Proposal

Project Theme

Soccer is the greatest sport on earth!

The game is governed by several rules, also known as the Laws of the Game (LOTG), that the referees, players, coaches and spectators need to be familiar with in order to conduct and enjoy the game.

For the Final Project I will be designing a soccer-related website "SoccerEd" that briefly describes the Laws of the Game in order to educate the site visitors.

Prime Objectives

As an educational website, SoccerEd invites the visitors to:

  • familiarize themselves with a summary of the LOTG,
  • test their knowledge of the LOTG, and
  • read the latest news related to the LOTG.

Target Audience

Soccer is played on every continent, in every country and at many different levels. The Laws of the Game are the same for all soccer matches throughout the world, from the FIFA World Cup through to a game between young children in a local park. Therefore, the ideal target audience for SoccerEd site are: soccer referees, players, coaches and spectators.

Soccer is the most popular game for people of all ages and gender regardless of their location and the language they speak.

Information Architecture

The Final Project will be a six well-styled, semantic and responsive HTML pages interlinked via a single navigation menu. The main navigation menu is prominently positioned on top of each page. All pages are further styled by one single external style sheet.

SoccerEd website is comprised of six pages titled:

  • Home - a homepage with welcoming message and describing the objectives of the site. This page also provides internal links to other main pages: LOTG, Quizzes and New.
  • LOTG - Laws of the Game page will provide an index for and the summaries of all 17 Laws of the Game.
  • Quizzes - the Quizzes page will host a monthly, short quiz on the Laws of the Game. It also contains the archived quizzes from the other months in the current calendar year.
  • News - the News page will publish the most recent news related to the Laws of the Game and covers other related news. It also provides a link to the archived news.
  • Lorem - the Lorem Ipsum page will contain the content provided by COMP 1850 course and will be styled along the rest of the site content.
  • Contact - the Contact page will provide the contact information for the Web Admin and provides the visitors with a contact form to reach the author of the site.
